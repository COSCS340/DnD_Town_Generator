Welcome to our town generator!

To load a premade seed:

  1. Open up the "Wizard" tab.
  2. Click the "Load" button on the right of the page
  3. Load in your file
  4. Assign a name to the town and a population size
  5. Click "Build Town"

 

To create a seed:

  1. Load in any files necessary
    • You must have at least one file specifying people (ex. "people.json")
    • You must have at least one file specifying classes of people (ex. "fisherman.json")
  2. Click "Generate Seed"
  3. Specify a location for the saved seed
  4. Assign your town a name and population
  5. Click "Build Town."
